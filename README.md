# **Arcturan**

**Arcturan+** is the Geode addon for the Geometry Dash level **Arcturan**, created by thejshadow and MoistenedEgg.

Immerse yourself in the world of Arcturan, where the Eternal Winter has laid rest upon the land. Fight your way through hordes of enemies, defeat twelve challenging bosses, and traverse through six handcrafted dungeons. Will you succeed in restoring warmth and light to the world once more?

**This mod adds many quality of life features to Arcturan:**

* Progress saving across sessions
* Platformer time saving

(These features have been approved by RobTop)
